The company has a registered capital of 10 million yuan, equipment assets of 60 million yuan, and its own factory building area of 10848 m2. It has a professional technical team, including 2 professor consultants, 3 associate professor experts, and more than 30 engineers and assistants with medium and senior professional titles.

The company’s existing lithium battery customization solutions and products are widely used in aerospace, satellite navigation, high-energy physics, railway infrastructure, public security, power communications, medical electronics, commercial finance, security communications, transportation and logistics, exploration and mapping, photovoltaic energy storage, 3C consumer electronics and other fields. The company meets the requirements of ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 systems.

Commercial & Industrial Energy Storage Battery

Commercial and industrial energy storage systems help businesses reduce electricity costs, improve power stability, and ensure reliable backup during outages.

They are widely used in different industries and applications:

Factories and manufacturing plants – reduce peak electricity costs and stabilize production
Warehouses and logistics centers – ensure uninterrupted operation of equipment and lighting
Office buildings and shopping malls – lower energy bills and provide backup power
Farms and irrigation systems – support water pumps and agricultural equipment
Solar + storage projects – store excess solar energy and use it at night
Construction sites – provide temporary and stable power supply
Telecom base stations – ensure continuous operation during power outages
Hospitals and data rooms – provide emergency backup for critical loads

Energy storage is becoming an essential solution for businesses seeking energy independence and cost savings.
